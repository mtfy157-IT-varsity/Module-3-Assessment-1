Omelette

Prep time: 20 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients









Steps

  1. Whisk together three eggs in a mixing bowl. Season with salt and plenty of pepper.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of butter in an omelette or small frying pan set over a moderate heat until hot.
  3. Add the beaten egg to the pan, tilting it to cover the surface of the pan. Leave the egg to set slightly before drawing the edges inwards from the perimeter, letting the uncooked egg run under.
  4. Add slices of avocado and feta on top, followed by a small handful of spinach. Flip the omelette over to cover the spinach. Cover the pan with a lid and leave for a minute. Remove the lid and sprinkle over some chopped chives.
